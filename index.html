Welcome to SandSeed Academy

At SandSeed Academy we offer a curriculum which provides student's with the necessary skills that are key to their growth and education. We have extradionary staff that is trained to be able to teach and take care of our learners.

We offer pre-school education to kids that reside in Ghanzi and surronding areas. At SandSeed our mission is to provide the community with affordable education that is necessary for their children's growth.

Did You Know?

SandSeed Academy was opened in late 2025 and has enrolled over 80 student's since it's start of operation

Services Offered at Sand Seed Academy

Indoor Learning

Education is delivered through visuals and word of mouth and it takes places within our classes.

Student Meals

We offer our student's with readily cooked meals to help them regain their energies and to also help in their growth

Outdoor Learning

We offer our student's with hand's on learning and this improves their creativity and their mobility skills
