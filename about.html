About Sand Seed Academy

Sand Seed Academy was built and founded in the year 2025 by a motswana man named Rapelang Monnaatlala.Sand Seed Academy was built with one mission in mind and that was to provide affordable high quality education to all the resident's that live in Ghanzi.

The main Goal for Ghanzi is to provide job opportunities as well as an institute where children can learn and grow in. At Sand Seed Academy we pride ourselves with upholding high quality education and delivering excellent care to our students. We have a staff that is professionally trained to take care and to also teach our learners. Our school curriculum includes meals and practical sessions for our students, we not only train their minds but we also train their bodies.
